what is this

anima2b is an ai image model made by circlestone labs in collaboration with comfy org, trained on millions of anime / art images including ~59k credited artists. this tool lets you check if your name was in that artist index.

this is the model in question: huggingface.co/circlestone-labs/Anima

if your art was used
if your name is in the list, there's honestly not a ton of recourse right now. copyright law around ai training data is still a huge mess and nothing is settled.

best thing you can do is document everything. screenshot the model page, save the artist index, note the date. and find other artists in the same situation and band together. the more people that can show their work was used, the louder the conversation gets.

communities like artists rights orgs, netbloc, and others have been collecting this kind of evidence. you're not alone in this.

if you need someone who is familiar with these things to consult for guidance, or for additional information, please add @berrycon_ on discord. i will try my best to give insight.
